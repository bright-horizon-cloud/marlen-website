About Marla home & décor

Marla home & décor is a full-service renovation, interior design, and décor studio with offices in Johannesburg and Lubumbashi. We are dedicated to transforming homes into beautiful, functional living environments — creating stylish, personalized spaces that reflect our clients' vision from concept to completion.

Whether it's a full renovation, modern interior design, or finishing décor touches, we bring creativity, quality, and attention to detail to every project.

Our Services

Renovation

From kitchen and bathroom remodels to full home transformations, we manage every detail for a seamless renovation experience.

Interior Design
